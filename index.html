About Me

Rebecca is a dynamic and expressive dancer specializing in contemporary and modern dance. With a passion for storytelling through movement, she has performed in various productions across the country, captivating audiences with her powerful and emotional performances. She holds a BFA in Dance from the Juilliard School and has trained with renowned choreographers. Rebecca's work explores themes of human connection, resilience, and the natural world, aiming to create profound and resonant experiences for viewers.
